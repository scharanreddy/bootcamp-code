# Industry Security Briefing — Financial Services

## Executive Briefing

Financial Services organizations should prioritize protection of Core banking systems, Trading platforms, and Customer financial records. The most relevant threat themes are Fraud, Ransomware, and Insider threats, requiring controls aligned to Protect transaction integrity, Strengthen identity and access controls, and Detect fraud quickly.

## Top Threats

- Fraud
- Ransomware
- Insider threats
- Advanced persistent threats

## Attack Patterns

- Business email compromise
- Credential theft
- API abuse
- DDoS attacks

## Recommended Controls

- Protect transaction integrity
- Strengthen identity and access controls
- Detect fraud quickly
- Meet regulatory requirements

## Business Priorities

- Operational disruption and service downtime
- Regulatory, contractual, or compliance exposure
- Loss of customer, citizen, patient, or partner trust
- Financial impact from incident response, fraud, recovery, and remediation
